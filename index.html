Ride to Biligirirangana Hills

The Biligiriranga Hills, commonly called B R Hills, is a hill range situated in south-eastern Karnataka, in South India. The area is called Biligiriranga Swamy Temple Wildlife Sanctuary. It is a protected reserve under the Wildlife Protection Act of 1972. Being at the confluence of the Western Ghats and the Eastern Ghats, the sanctuary is home to eco-systems that are unique to both the mountain ranges. The site was declared a Tiger Reserve in December 2010.

The Himalayan Hills

This epic journey is the very essence of the “High Himalayas” and we are going at it for the second time – this time with a difference. We take some of the less explored trails of this beautiful land to make this epic journey unique and memorable. Traversing some of the most spectacular landscapes on the planet, including one of the most dangerous roads in the world, every kilometre is breathtaking. Starting our journey in the pristine valleys and beautiful lakes of Kashmir we go on to some offbeat and lesser taken routes through the mesmerising dry mountain topography of Ladakh with its high altitude lakes kissing the borders of Pakistan and China and finally ending in the lower Himalayas in Himachal. Riding on this 17 day adventure it is going to be a one of a kind experience. And mind it, this is not your regular Ladakh Ride
